About Us

Broadcast Centre is a division of Solution Centre that focuses on Audio Visual, Production and Broadcast Equipment. We have gained experience over the years in our unique Zimbabwean market and feel that we can offer not only the best solutions for your needs but also great advice.

The division came about when we were approached by a local up and coming TV station that needed to have a functioning station in a matter of months and on a very tight budget. Solution Centre took on the challenge with help from some of our International Consultants and were able to meet the deadline. From there we have cemented our relationships with brands like BlackMagic, Sennheiser, Yamaha, Saramonic, Softron, SNS Evo, Fortinge, SWIT and LiveU, to mention a few, and have continued growing these brands in Zimbabwe as well as being able to support our customers.
