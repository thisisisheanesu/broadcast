Services

Consultancy

We have consulted on a number of studio projects as well as TV Station startups. We offer consultancy on equipment, premises, sound treatment, work flows as well as scheduling for both the Radio and TV markets as well as for Product Houses. We have International consultants who we work with and are able to recommend for more in depth business proposals and day to day running.

Systems Design

In some situations our customers have leant on us to assist with designing and building their studio and equipment needs based on the product or content they want to be able to create or produce for their consumers. From these discussions we then produce an in depth System Drawing and Bill of Materials for our customers.

Systems Integration

After the Systems Design phase the equipment will then need to be deployed and installed. We offer this as a service and will do everything from cabling and crimping to equipment installation and commissioning.
